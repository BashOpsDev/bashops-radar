What do I get on Free?

What do I get on Pro?

Repository discovery, unlimited analyses, founder outreach, unlimited pipeline history, and CSV export.

No. BashOps Radar helps you find better opportunities and prepare stronger outreach, but outcomes depend on execution and market timing.
