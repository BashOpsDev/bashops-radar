Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 1, 2026

Passwords are stored using secure hashing. We do not store plain-text passwords.

Payment processing is handled by third-party payment providers. BashOps Radar does not store full card details.

We take reasonable steps to protect user data, including authentication, environment secrets, database access controls, and secure hosting practices.
