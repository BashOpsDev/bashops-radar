Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 1, 2026

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information BashOps Radar collects, how it is used, and how it is protected.

1. Information We Collect

When you create an account, we may collect:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Encrypted password
  • Subscription status
  • Repository analysis history
  • Pipeline activity

2. GitHub Repository Analysis

When you analyze a repository, BashOps Radar temporarily processes public GitHub repository information such as repository metadata, issues, pull requests, programming languages, stars, forks, and activity signals to generate recommendations.

3. AI Features

Some features use third-party AI providers to generate summaries, recommendations, and developer outreach suggestions. These providers process only the information required to generate the requested results.

4. Cookies & Sessions

We use secure session cookies to keep users signed in and protect authenticated areas of the platform.

5. Payments

Payments are processed by our payment provider. BashOps Radar never stores your full payment card information.

6. Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect user data, including encrypted passwords, secure sessions, environment variables, database protections, and HTTPS hosting.

7. Third-Party Services

BashOps Radar may integrate with services such as:

  • GitHub API
  • Google Gemini API
  • Paddle
  • Railway
  • PostgreSQL

8. Your Rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your account information by contacting us.

9. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy as BashOps Radar evolves. Updates will be published on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date.
