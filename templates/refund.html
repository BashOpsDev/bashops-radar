Refund Policy

Last Updated: July 1, 2026

This Refund Policy explains how subscription cancellations and refunds are handled for BashOps Radar.

1. Subscription Billing

BashOps Radar Pro is billed on a recurring monthly basis unless cancelled before the next renewal date.

2. Cancellation

You may cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings or by contacting us. After cancellation, your Pro features remain available until the end of your current billing period.

3. Refund Eligibility

Because BashOps Radar provides immediate access to digital software and AI services, subscription payments are generally non-refundable once a billing period has started.

However, refunds may be considered in situations such as:

  • Duplicate charges
  • Accidental payments
  • Verified billing errors
  • Technical issues that prevented access to the paid service

4. Free Plan

Users can continue using BashOps Radar under the Free plan, subject to its feature and usage limits, without providing payment information.

5. Chargebacks

If you believe a payment was made in error, please contact us before filing a chargeback. We will work with you to resolve billing issues as quickly as possible.

6. Policy Updates

We may update this Refund Policy as our services evolve. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
