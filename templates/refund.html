Refund Policy

Last updated: July 1, 2026

This Refund Policy explains how refunds and cancellations work for BashOps Radar subscriptions.

1. Monthly Subscriptions

BashOps Radar Pro is billed monthly. Users may cancel their subscription at any time.

2. Cancellation

After cancellation, Pro access may remain active until the end of the current billing period.

3. Refunds

We generally do not provide refunds for a billing period that has already started. However, refunds may be considered for duplicate charges, accidental billing errors, or serious technical issues that prevent access to paid features.

4. Free Plan

Users may continue using the Free plan without payment, subject to Free plan limits.
